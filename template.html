Love & Coding

Who I Am

My name is Alexis, and I use she/her or they/them pronouns. Just like this website, my life is chock-full of love and code, and I like to share as much of both as I can. With those things I hope to make the world a better place, even if just a little.

Love as Input

Here's a few things I love

Reading

I love to read, and usually go through about 50-60 books a year. These days I mostly do audiobooks, a habit I picked up from when I used to have long commutes

Theater

Going to Broadway shows is one of my most favorite ways to spend a night out. I'm especially a sucker for musicals.

Games

For a night in, playing games with friends or my partner is just a wonderful way to spend time. And I play games of both the board and video varieties.

Learning

Learning and trying new things is always an adventure. I love to try my hand at new projects or skills so that I can grow and improve.

Myself

It took a long time for me to come to terms with myself, but I've finally learned to love myself. So much so, that I wrote myself (and the world) a letter about it.
